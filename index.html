Who We Are

Atmagaurav Infra Private Limited

Atmagaurav Infra Private Limited is a leading government infrastructure contractor headquartered in Bhayander East, Thane, Maharashtra. Since our inception in 2009, we have been delivering critical infrastructure projects that connect communities and drive economic growth across India.

We specialize in highway construction, bridge engineering, road maintenance, and road safety systems — all executed to the highest standards set by NHAI, MORTH, IRC, and State PWD.

Our team of experienced engineers, project managers, and skilled professionals brings deep domain expertise and a passion for excellence to every project we undertake.

What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive infrastructure solutions delivered with precision, quality, and safety.

01 — Highway Construction

End-to-End Highway Construction

We deliver complete highway construction from land acquisition support to final commissioning — greenfield expressways, upgradation, four-laning, six-laning, and related civil works.

  • ✓Greenfield & brownfield highway development
  • ✓Four-laning & six-laning of national highways
  • ✓Flexible & rigid pavement construction
  • ✓MORTH & IRC code compliant execution
02 — Bridge Construction

Bridge & Flyover Engineering

Our bridge engineering team handles design coordination, construction, and commissioning of major bridges, river crossings, viaducts, foot over bridges, and flyovers.

  • ✓RCC & PSC bridge construction
  • ✓Foot over bridges (FOB) & pedestrian underpasses
  • ✓Flyovers, ROBs & grade separators
  • ✓IRC & IS code compliant design
03 — Highway Maintenance

Periodic & Routine Maintenance

We provide comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for national and state highways — ensuring roads remain safe, pothole-free, and functional throughout their service life.

  • ✓Annual maintenance contracts (AMC)
  • ✓Pothole patching & bituminous overlay
  • ✓Drainage maintenance & clearance
  • ✓Emergency monsoon repairs
04 — Road Safety Works

Safety Systems & Signage

We install, maintain, and audit comprehensive road safety systems that reduce accidents and protect lives on national and state highways.

  • ✓W-beam & thrie-beam crash barriers
  • ✓Retro-reflective road signage & delineators
  • ✓Thermoplastic road markings
  • ✓Safety audits & black-spot treatment
